Traditional Salvadoran Cuisine

Salvadoran cuisine is flavorful, comforting, and deeply rooted in tradition. It often features ingredients like corn, beans, cheese, plantains, and meat. Many recipes are passed down through generations and are central to family gatherings and national celebrations.
